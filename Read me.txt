Click Me If You Can – Reaction Speed Game

A fast-paced visual reaction game where players must quickly identify and click the one red square among green ones.
The faster and more accurately you react, the higher your score.



How to play

Click Play

A grid of green squares appears

One square turns red

Click the red square as fast as you can

Score increases based on speed

Clicking a green square gives a penalty

If you do nothing, the round auto-fails

Press STOP anytime to view results




Game Rules Implemented
Odd One Out =Exactly 1 red square appears every round

Correct click=Click the odd one→ score increases

Wrong click= Click green → score decreases

Reaction time=Faster click → more points

Timeout=No click in time → counts as wrong

Accuracy=Tracks correct vs total attempts
